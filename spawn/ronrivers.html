Ron Rivers Auto Group

Location Overview

Ron Rivers Auto Group is a place in Greenville where players can purchase vehicles and role-play with friends or other players. It is owned by the wealthy businessman and Falcon Prime junkie, Ron Rivers. This place claims to sell only used cars, but they also do other things, like detail your cars.

This is the first ever competitor against Roadmap, but people still prefer Roadmap because of the size comparison.

Some rooms were removed in Version 1.55.0 for optimization purposes.

Layout

The building is large in terms of size and big in shape. The exterior is painted red, white, and blue, the colors of the American flag. There are two prop cars currently located outside of the building, which are switched out every so often. At the front of the building, there's a red 2007 Falcon Prime with the license plate "F-GYUNG" on display. If you go to the back of the building, there's a parking spot labeled "Ron's Spot" as a designated parking spot for only Ron Rivers himself. Going inside, there's a nice big main room with seats, desks, chairs, Ron's Office, and a waiting area with a TV. Inside of Ron's Office is Ron's desk and office chair, along with papers, shelves, and 18 Best Employee plaques. Going through the glass doors will lead you to the F&I (Financing & Insurance) office. There's an employee break room with a coffee maker, a vending machine, some pictures, a couch, and chairs. There is also a detailing bay for role-playing, so you can detail other player's vehicles. There are also Men and Women's restrooms, and a huge service station located in the back of the building, where you can customize your vehicles.
